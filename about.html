About

A Forest of Greens is a digital color study exploring the psychological and historical narrative of nature’s primary palette. This project serves as an interactive field guide, taking the viewer on a deliberate journey from the grounded permanence of the forest floor to the vibrant energy of new life.

The Narrative Journey

Stability

Representing Ancient Strength, this phase focuses on deep, enduring tones like Deep Forest Green (#0E550A) that anchor the visual landscape.

Harmony

Representing Composed Calm, this phase explores balanced, restorative mid-tones like Moss Green (#496415) that symbolize organic resilience.

Growth

Representing Vital Expansion, this phase highlights high-energy shades like Fresh Spring Green (#B2D824) that capture the forward momentum of a forest in bloom.
